Pillar Table

Functional Art Table

The form is defined by strict geometry—only straight lines and right angles. Yet within that rigid language, the vertical grooves map the plotting points of an ellipse. A curved projection, composed entirely of linear elements. It’s a study in contrast—precision versus softness, logic versus illusion.

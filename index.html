about me

I am a Professional Joiner.
Furniture Design and Manufacturing is my Métier.

Eukleídēs translates from Ancient Greek to "Glorius/Renowned". This was the name given to Greek Mathematician Euclid, the father of geometry. I apply the very principles he discovered in every project, ensuring to design and manufacture them in the most stable and visually pleasing manner possible. Exellence is my standard.
